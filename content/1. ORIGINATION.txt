CoronaVirus
CoronaViruses are a family of viruses discovered in the 20th century, this can cause respirtory targeted diseases like
common cold and even lethal diseases like SARS, MERS and the present pandemic Covid19(It is to be noted that Covid19 is a 
CoronaVirus but every coronavirus isnt Covid19). It is also important to know that a vaccine for the same is yet to be 
developed.
Now ill talk about Covid19 it is also commonly reffered to as Novel Coronavirus. It was first discovered first in December 
2019 in Wuhan, China(thus the reference of 19 in its name) and thereafter it has been spreading rapidly causing this 
PANDEMIC.
